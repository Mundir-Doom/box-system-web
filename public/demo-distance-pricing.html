Distance-Based Pricing System

Revolutionary pricing system for your courier business

Key Features

Distance Calculation

Automatic calculation of delivery distance using GPS coordinates and Haversine formula.

Dynamic Pricing

Real-time pricing based on actual distance, weight, and hub transfer charges.

Hub Transfers

Automatic calculation of transfer charges between different hubs.

Weight Multiplier

Optional weight factor that affects the final pricing calculation.

How It Works

1Select Pricing Method

Choose between "Fixed Pricing" (traditional) or "Distance-Based Pricing" (new system).

2Set Locations

Use the map to set pickup and delivery locations. The system automatically captures GPS coordinates.

3Configure Options

Select origin hub, transfer hub (optional), and enter weight (optional).

4Calculate Distance

Click "Calculate Distance" to get real-time distance and pricing information.

5View Breakdown

See detailed pricing breakdown including distance, base rate, weight multiplier, and transfer charges.

6Create Parcel

Submit the form with accurate distance-based pricing automatically calculated.
